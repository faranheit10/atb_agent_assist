COMMON CUSTOMER QUESTIONS — CREDIT CARDS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Q: How do I activate my new ATB credit card?
A: Call 1-866-904-9677 OR activate through ATB Personal online banking or the ATB Personal app.

Q: How do I lock/unlock my card if I think it's been compromised?
A: Log in to ATB Personal app or web → select the card → use the lock/unlock toggle. Call 1-800-332-8383 for additional support.

Q: When is my cash back applied?
A: Cash back rewards on the Gold Cash Rewards card are automatically applied to the account every January.

Q: Do my ATB My Rewards points ever expire?
A: No. ATB My Rewards points never expire and have no blackout dates.

Q: Can I increase my credit card limit?
A: Yes. Contact ATB Client Care at 1-800-332-8383 or visit a branch. A credit review will be completed.

Q: What is the minimum monthly payment on my ATB Mastercard?
A: The minimum monthly payment includes the interest charges and a portion of the balance. Payments made reduce the balance; multiple payments per month are allowed and do not negatively affect your credit score.

Q: What happens when I make a credit card payment?
A: Payments go toward the balance — after interest charges and fees are paid. ATB recommends paying the full balance each month to avoid interest.

Q: What is a cash advance on my credit card?
A: A cash advance is withdrawing cash using your credit card at an ATM. It typically carries a higher interest rate (21.90%) than purchases, and interest starts accruing immediately with no grace period.

Q: What is a chargeback and when can I request one?
A: A chargeback disputes a charge on your credit card — for example, if a merchant charged you incorrectly or if you didn't receive what you paid for. Contact ATB Client Care to initiate. Not all transactions qualify. ATB will investigate the dispute with Mastercard.

Q: Can I put my ATB Mastercard on my phone for tap payments?
A: Yes. ATB Mastercards work with Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Samsung Pay. Set up through your phone's wallet app.
