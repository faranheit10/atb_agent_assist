SECTION A: CHEQUING ACCOUNTS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

ATB offers chequing accounts for every life stage. All accounts include:
- ATB Personal online and mobile banking access (free)
- Visa Debit-enabled debit card for in-store and online purchases
- Mobile pay support: Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay
- 180-day transaction history viewable in ATB Personal
- Interac e-Transfer sending and receiving capability
- ATB TrackIt budgeting tool

IMPORTANT FEE NOTE (effective August 1, 2024):
- Non-Sufficient Funds (NSF) fee reduced from $38 to $10 on personal accounts.
- Paper statement fee now applies — clients encouraged to switch to e-statements.

───────────────────────────────────────
1. UNLIMITED CHEQUING ACCOUNT
───────────────────────────────────────
Best for: Clients who want unlimited transactions with no per-transaction fees.

Monthly fee: $15.95
Fee waived if:
  - Client maintains a minimum daily balance of $4,000 throughout the month, OR
  - Post-secondary student aged 26+ with proof of enrollment

Transactions: Unlimited (all types — in-branch, online, ABM, phone)
Interac e-Transfers: Included (unlimited)

Overdraft Protection: Available by application (approved credit required)
  - Interest rate: 17.25% per annum on daily overdrawn balance
  - Minimum charge: $4.00 or monthly interest, whichever is greater

ABM (ATM) access:
  - Within Alberta: Free at ATB ABMs; non-ATB ABM fees apply (client notified first)
  - Outside Alberta (within Canada): ATB reimburses up to $4.50 per withdrawal, up to 2 withdrawals/month
  - Outside Canada: $5.00 per withdrawal (Cirrus® network); ATB reimburses $8 for 1 US withdrawal/month

Additional perks: None specific to this tier

───────────────────────────────────────
2. ATB ADVANTAGE ACCOUNT
───────────────────────────────────────
Best for: Clients who want premium perks, credit card fee waivers, and rate discounts.

Monthly fee: Not specified on page; fee waiver with minimum $6,000 monthly balance
Transactions: Unlimited

Key perks:
  - Annual fee waiver on ATB World Elite Mastercard® ($120 value) and ATB US Dollar Mastercard ($19 value)
  - Interest rate reduction of up to 2.5% on approved personal lines of credit (minimum rate: Prime + 3%)
  - Free Alberta Lifestyle cheques, bank drafts, and stop payments
  - $12 discount off annual safety deposit box fee
  - Overdraft Protection available (apply separately)

ABM access:
  - Outside Alberta (within Canada): reimburses up to $4.50/withdrawal for up to 5 withdrawals/month
  - ABM withdrawals outside Canada: 2.5% above Mastercard exchange rate

Mastercard rates on this account:
  - Standard purchase rate: 19.90%
  - Cash advance rate: 21.90%

Joint account: Yes, can be opened jointly; applications available online.

───────────────────────────────────────
3. NO-FEE ALL-IN DIGITAL ACCOUNT
───────────────────────────────────────
Best for: Clients who bank digitally and want a no-fee account bundled with a line of credit.

Monthly fee: $0 (no monthly fee)
Eligibility: Must qualify for attached line of credit (credit check required)

Line of credit included: $5,000 – $50,000 (approved credit)
  - Interest rate: Variable, based on ATB prime rate
  - Minimum monthly payment: Interest only on outstanding balance
  - No fixed repayment term (revolving credit)

Free transactions: All online banking, ABM, bill payments, transfers, mobile pay, mobile cheque deposit, balance inquiries, telephone banking

Fee applies:
  - $5 per in-branch or Client Care phone-assisted transaction
  - $5 for cashed cheques
  - $1.25 for manual phone bill payments

Visa Debit card included: Yes, for online and in-store purchases at no additional fee

Note: Requires existing credit relationship — if client cannot qualify for the LOC, they should consider another chequing account.

───────────────────────────────────────
4. FREEDOM ACCOUNT (Age 59+)
───────────────────────────────────────
Best for: Clients aged 59 and older who want unlimited transactions with no monthly fee.

Monthly fee: $0
Transactions: Unlimited
Additional perks: Premium service; USD banking available; access to ATB expert advice

Eligibility: Must be 59 years of age or older

───────────────────────────────────────
5. GENERATION ACCOUNT (Ages 25 and Under)
───────────────────────────────────────
Best for: Young Albertans (students, workers, gap year) who want free banking.

Monthly fee: $0
Transactions: Unlimited (save, spend, transfer)
Eligibility: Must be 25 years of age or younger; Alberta resident

Notes:
  - Previously offered as "Students First Account" — existing Students First holders aged 25 and under were migrated to Generation Account (August 2024).
  - Students First holders aged 26+ should contact ATB to find the right account.
  - Minimum investment amounts on GICs for Generation Account holders: $100 (vs $1,000 standard)

───────────────────────────────────────
6. BASIC ACCOUNT
───────────────────────────────────────
Best for: Clients who need low-cost banking with minimal transactions.

Monthly fee: $3.95
Included transactions: 15 debit transactions per month

Per-transaction fees after limit:
  - In-branch withdrawal or transfer out: $1.25
  - Cheque: $1.25
  - Electronic withdrawal or transfer (incl. Global Transfers): $1.25
  - Bill payment: $1.25

───────────────────────────────────────
7. PAY AS YOU GO ACCOUNT
───────────────────────────────────────
Best for: Clients with 4 or fewer monthly transactions (very infrequent use).

Monthly fee: $0
Transactions: Pay per use (no monthly fee, no included free transactions)

Per-transaction fees:
  - In-branch withdrawal or transfer out: $1.75
  - Cheque: $1.75
  - Electronic withdrawal or transfer (incl. Global Transfers): $1.75
  - Bill payment: $2.00

───────────────────────────────────────
US DOLLAR CHEQUING (Add-on)
───────────────────────────────────────
USD Banking available for clients who need to spend, save, and access credit in US dollars.
No monthly fee for existing clients holding Unlimited Account, ATB Advantage Account, Freedom Account, or Basic Account.
Available for other clients at applicable monthly rate.
ABM withdrawals outside Canada: converted at 2.5% above Mastercard exchange rate.
Transactions charged the applicable exchange rate on the day processed (not the day initiated).

───────────────────────────────────────
1. SPRINGBOARD SAVINGS ACCOUNT
───────────────────────────────────────
Best for: Clients who want to earn interest on savings with easy access and no minimum balance.

Monthly fee: $0
Minimum balance: None
Interest: Tiered, calculated daily, paid monthly on last day of month
  - Tier 1: Balance below $5,000
  - Tier 2: Balance $5,000 to below $250,000 (earns more)
  - Tier 3: Balance $250,000 and above

Base interest rate: Up to 0.70%
Current promotion (January 12 – March 31, 2026): Up to 4.70% total (base + 4.00% bonus on net new deposits up to $1,000,000)
Bonus interest applies to net new funds only (increase over starting balance as of Jan 12, 2026)

Free transactions: Deposits and transfers to/from ATB accounts
Transaction fee: $5 per transaction (after the 1 free per month)

Important: Designed for saving, not spending. Clients who need frequent transactions should also open a chequing account.

Can be linked to Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay — but NOT recommended for everyday spending due to per-transaction fees.

COMMON CUSTOMER QUESTIONS ON SAVINGS:
───────────────────────────────────────
Q: Can I withdraw from my savings account anytime?
A: Yes, but if you withdraw more than once per month from a Springboard Savings Account, a $5 fee applies to each additional transaction. Deposits and transfers to/from ATB accounts are free.

Q: Is my money safe at ATB?
A: Yes. ATB Financial is owned by the Government of Alberta. Deposits with ATB are fully guaranteed by the Province of Alberta (not CDIC — ATB is not a CDIC member, but the Government of Alberta guarantee is considered equivalent protection for Albertans).

Q: Can I open a savings account online?
A: Yes, via ATB Personal web or ATB Personal app.

Q: What is ATB TrackIt?
A: ATB TrackIt is a money management tool built into ATB Personal that helps clients track spending, create budgets, set debt repayment goals, and see their full financial picture — including investments held at other institutions.
